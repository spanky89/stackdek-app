# StackDek Onboarding Video Script

**Length:** 5 minutes  
**Tone:** Casual, authentic, contractor-to-contractor  
**Tool:** Loom (loom.com - free, easy)

---

## 📝 Script Outline:

### [0:00-0:30] Hook & Intro
```
"Hey, I'm Jarrod. I run a landscaping business here in North Georgia, 
and I built StackDek because every CRM out there is designed for 
corporate teams with office staff - not contractors working out of 
a truck.

Let me show you how it actually works when you're using it every day."
```

**What to show:**
- Your face on camera (quick intro)
- Then switch to screen share of StackDek dashboard

---

### [0:30-1:30] The Stack (Core Feature)

```
"This is my job stack. Instead of a rigid calendar with time blocks, 
it's just a pile of jobs - the way I actually think about my day.

Top of the pile? That's what I'm doing first. Rain delay? I just 
drag this job down. Emergency call comes in? Drag it to the top.

No complicated rescheduling, no recalculating time slots. Just... 
drag and drop. That's it."
```

**What to show:**
- Your actual job stack (8-10 jobs)
- Drag a job from position 3 to position 1
- Drag another job down
- Show how simple it is

---

### [1:30-2:30] 60-Second Quoting (The Killer Feature)

```
"Here's the thing that changed my business: I quote jobs ON THE SPOT.

I'm standing in a customer's yard right now - well, not literally, 
but you get it. They want a patio. I pull out my phone, tap 'New Quote.'

[Click through the quote creation process]

Customer name... line items... boom. Quote sent via text before I 
even walk to my truck.

Customers are SHOCKED. They're used to contractors saying 'I'll 
email you something next week' and then ghosting them. When you 
quote on the spot, you look different. You close more jobs."
```

**What to show:**
- Click "New Quote"
- Fill out form FAST (show it's actually quick)
- Enter 3-4 line items
- Click "Send via Text"
- Show the quote preview/sent confirmation

---

### [2:30-3:30] Mobile-First (Why It Matters)

```
"Because here's the reality: I'm never at a desk. I'm on a roof, 
in a yard, in my truck.

So this whole thing works perfectly on my phone.
[Switch to mobile view or show on actual phone]

Same features. Same data. Add a job, check a task, send a quote - 
all from my phone. That's the whole point."
```

**What to show:**
- Mobile responsive view in browser (shrink window)
- OR hold up your phone and show the app
- Navigate: job stack → clients → quotes
- Show it's fully functional, not a crippled mobile version

---

### [3:30-4:30] Jobs & Tasks (Staying Organized)

```
"Every job has tasks attached. Like this patio job:
- Order pavers
- Rent compactor
- Confirm fire pit with customer

I just check them off as I go. Nothing fancy. Just keeps me from 
forgetting the dumb stuff that costs me time or money.

And when a job's done, I mark it complete. Next job auto-highlights. 
Simple flow."
```

**What to show:**
- Click into a job detail page
- Show the task list
- Check off a task
- Mark job as complete
- Show next job in stack highlights

---

### [4:30-5:00] Wrap & CTA

```
"That's StackDek. I built it for my business first, and now I'm 
sharing it with other contractors who are tired of overpaying for 
bloated software they don't need.

Try it free for 14 days. No credit card. If it doesn't save you 
time in the first week, don't use it.

But I think you're gonna love it. Let's go build something."
```

**What to show:**
- Back to dashboard (full view)
- Quick pan across the features
- End on a confident note

## 🎬 Recording Checklist:

**Before you hit record:**
- [ ] Close all other browser tabs
- [ ] Hide bookmarks bar (Ctrl+Shift+B)
- [ ] Open StackDek in full screen
- [ ] Make sure your account has real-looking data (8-10 jobs, clients, quotes)
- [ ] Test your mic (say a few words, listen back)
- [ ] Close Slack, email, anything that might ding

**During recording:**
- [ ] Don't rush - speak at normal pace
- [ ] Pause between sections (easier to edit)
- [ ] If you mess up, just pause, then start that sentence over
- [ ] Show, don't just tell (click things, navigate)
- [ ] Keep it real - you're talking to a fellow contractor, not a boardroom

**After recording:**
- [ ] Watch it back (does it make sense?)
- [ ] Trim dead air at start/end
- [ ] Upload to YouTube as "Unlisted"
- [ ] Copy embed URL
- [ ] Add to StackDek app

---

## 💡 Pro Tips:

**Energy matters:** You're excited about this product. Let that show. Doesn't have to be over-the-top, just... alive.

**Be yourself:** Your personality is the differentiator. Corporate CRMs have slick videos. You have authenticity.

**Speed is a feature:** Show how FAST you can quote. That's the killer feature. Emphasize it.

**One take is fine:** Don't agonize over perfection. Contractors don't want a polished corporate video. They want to see that this was made by someone who actually does the work.

## 🎥 Loom Quick Start:

1. Go to loom.com
2. Install Chrome extension
3. Click Loom icon in toolbar
4. Choose "Screen + Camera"
5. Select your browser window
6. Hit "Start Recording"
7. You'll see a 3-2-1 countdown
8. Start talking!
9. When done, click "Stop"
10. Loom auto-uploads
11. Copy the share link
12. Paste into StackDek app

**Embed code format:**
```
https://www.loom.com/embed/[VIDEO_ID]
```
